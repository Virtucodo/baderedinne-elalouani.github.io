Banana Pancakes

Banana pancakes are a delicious and easy breakfast treat. They are made by mixing together a batter of flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt, and then stirring in milk, an egg, melted butter, and mashed banana. The batter is then spooned onto a hot griddle or pan and cooked until bubbles form on the surface, then flipped and cooked until the other side is golden brown. Banana pancakes are typically served warm, with butter and syrup, and are a favorite of kids and adults alike. They are a great way to use up ripe bananas and make a tasty and satisfying breakfast or breakfast-for-dinner option.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

Step 1

In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

Step 2

In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter.

Step 3

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.

Step 4

Gently fold in the mashed banana.

Step 5

Heat a nonstick pan or griddle over medium heat.

Step 6

Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan for each pancake.

Step 7

Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake, then flip and cook until the other side is golden brown.

Step 8

Repeat with the remaining batter.

Step 9

Serve the pancakes warm, with butter and syrup. Enjoy!

ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour

2 tablespoons sugar

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/2 teaspoon baking soda

1/4 teaspoon salt

1 cup milk

1 egg

2 tablespoons butter, melted

1 medium banana, mashed

tools

Mixing bowls

Whisk

Measuring cups and spoons

Griddle or large nonstick pan

Spatula

Ladle or large spoon for scooping the batter onto the griddle
